🌍 Why Visit Nepal?

Nepal is a land of mountains, culture, peace, and adventure. From Everest to ancient temples, every place tells a story.

नेपाल हिमाल, संस्कृति र साहसिक यात्राको देश हो। यहाँको हरेक स्थानले कथा सुनाउँछ।

🏔️ Top Places

Pokhara 🌊

Peaceful lakes, mountain views, adventure sports.

पोखरा शान्त र सुन्दर शहर हो।

Kathmandu 🏙️

Temples, culture, history and busy city life.

काठमाडौं संस्कृति र इतिहासले भरिएको शहर हो।

Everest 🏔️

The highest mountain in the world.

सगरमाथा संसारकै अग्लो हिमाल हो।

Lumbini 🕊️

Birthplace of Buddha, peaceful and spiritual.

लुम्बिनी बुद्धको जन्मस्थान हो।

📸 Experience Nepal

🎎 Nepali Culture

Nepal is rich in traditions, festivals, and hospitality. People are warm and welcoming.

नेपाल विविध संस्कृति र चाडपर्वले भरिएको देश हो।

🍛 Nepali Food

Momos, Dal Bhat, Sekuwa, Newari dishes are must try foods.

मोमो, दालभात, सेकुवा अवश्य चाख्नुहोस्।

🐐 Nature & Animals

Nepal is home to yaks, goats, rhinos, and wildlife.

नेपालमा याक, बाख्रा र वन्यजन्तु पाइन्छन्।
